The Rock

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is a semi-retired professional wrestler, actor, and retired college football player.



Football

The Rock's career started as a college football player. He was approached by many Division 1 programs, and decided to recieve full scholarship to the University of Miami where he played as a defensive tackle for the Miami Hurricanes. Hower, his football career in college was cut short when he recieved an injury that forced him to be sidelined as well as replaced by Warren Sapp, a now football hall of fame player. After his college education, Dwayne Joined the Calgary Stampeders in the CFL (Canadian Football League), however was cut from the team after two months. Dwayne's football career is far from a success, but later on in life he would find himself new success in the form of Professional Wrestling.



Wrestling

Wrestling came naturally fo the rock, as his farthe and grandfather both participated in the sport. After his attempt at football, Johnson wanted to get in on the "family business". After successfully getting a contract to the WWF, Dwayne started going under the stage name "Rocky Maivia" and was hyped as the WWF's first 3rd generation wrestler. Sometime in the in his early career, Johnson started referring tp himself in the 3rd person as "The Rock". "The Rock has been listed as one of the all-time greatest professional wrestlers as well as one of the top box office draws in wrestling history. Hulk Hogan called The Rock "the biggest superstar in this business", and 16-time world champion John Cena described him as "the biggest superstar in the history of WWE". In "Cable Visions: Television Beyond Broadcasting", The Rock was described as 'for a long time, the WWE's biggest star and probably held the greatest international appeal'. R.D. Reynolds stated in his book "The WrestleCrap Book of Lists" that The Rock was 'the biggest star for WWE from 1999 until 2004'. Many wrestlers placed The Rock on their 'Mount Rushmore of Wrestling' including Hulk Hogan, Ric Flair, Chris Jericho and John Cena." - Wikipedia



Film

The Rock's film career started because of his popularity in the WWF/WWE. In the beginning he started acting primarily in action movies such as "The Scorpion King" and "The Rundown" But in recent years, Johnson as expanded to roles outside of his comfort zone. In the world of film, Dwayne gained notability for reinvigorating film franchises such as G.I. Joe and Fast & Furious where he played the characters Roadblock and Luke Hobbs respectively. Johnson has been nominated for numerous "Kid's Choice Awards" as well was named the top-grossing actor of 2013 for his portrayal of Luke Hobbs in Fast & Furious 6. In 2016, Johnson took his film career to youtube by starting a YouTube channel titled "The Rock" with the help of online personality Lilly Singh. His first video was called "The YouTube Factory" and featured several internet stars. He was featured in the site's yearly video series "YouTube Rewind" in 2016. Recently, The rock has starred in many blockbuster movies such as "Jumanji", "Baywatch", "The Fate of the Furious", and the Disney produced animated movie "Moana".
